About

A Sentiment Analysis-based Consumer Confidence Index (CCI) for Purchasing Behavior involves analyzing textual data, like social media posts and reviews, to gauge consumer sentiment. The process includes data collection, text preprocessing, sentiment analysis, scoring, aggregation, normalization, and index calculation. The resulting CCI provides a numerical representation of consumer confidence over time, helping to predict trends in purchasing behavior. This approach enables businesses and policymakers to understand and respond to shifts in consumer sentiment, facilitating more informed decision-making

Purpose

Our mission is to perform normalization, compute CCI values and apply ML algorithms and further more the project aims to use natural language processing and machine learning techniques to classify the sentiment of the reviews as positive, negative, or neutral. Compute the CCI for the industry or market segment based on the sentiment analysis of the consumer reviews. Provide insights and recommendations to businesses and policymakers based on the findings of the analysis.
